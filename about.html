ABOUT

The Junkyard Cafe is a small chain of casual dining resto-bars and lounges offering a rustic yet vibrant ambiance along with an eclectic bar menu serving an array of drinks and spirits making it a perfect place for people to enjoy the nightlife. The place offers an extensive food menu which is in line with the varied tastes and preferences of the diners and serves a variety of delicious Indian, Asian, and Mediterranean delicacies. The exclusive Hookah menu offers a range of flavorful and aromatic Sheeshas. This outlet is located in N Block, Connaught Place. The lounge provides a quirky environment with casual, colorful, and comfortable lounge and chairs. The additional feature is the beautiful open terrace area where one can have sumptuous food and amazing drinks while enjoying a spectacular view of the city
